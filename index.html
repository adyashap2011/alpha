How to help us?

Let us guide you through the basic ideas on how to donate or expand your business

Donate

Extra food at home? Better to give it away than throw it. Support families and underprivileged kids by giving them free food. Choose "free" to donate your food from the Sell Page.

Sell

Have a business? We understand how big brands could be asking for commission for your business. Put your prices to sell your food and we got you.

Buy

Discover sellers and their menu list. Add food items to your cart from the shop page. Got no cash? Don't worry! We also provide free food for people who can't afford.

Share

Our idea is to support businesses and families who are suffering, hence we would be very grateful if you could add more people to the community by telling them about our services.
